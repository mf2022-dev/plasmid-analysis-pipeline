# PlasmidFinder Replicon Typing Results - Key Findings

## 8 Complete ST11 K. pneumoniae Genomes (Long-Read)

### Conserved Plasmid Architecture (Present in ALL 8 complete isolates):

#### Plasmid 1: Large Conjugative Plasmid (~330-363 kb)
- **Replicon types:** IncFIB(pNDM-Mar) + IncHI1B(pNDM-MAR)
- **Identity:** 99.5%
- **Present in:** 8/8 isolates (100%)
- **Significance:** This is the pNDM-MAR-like plasmid - a major carbapenemase-carrying plasmid in ST11

#### Plasmid 2: Medium IncFII/IncR Multireplicon Plasmid (~130 kb)
- **Replicon types:** IncFII(pHN7A8) + IncR (primary), plus multiple IncFII variants
- **Identity:** 99-100%
- **Present in:** 8/8 isolates (100%)
- **Significance:** Multi-replicon plasmid likely carrying AMR genes

#### Plasmid 3: Small ColRNAI Plasmid (~10 kb)
- **Replicon type:** ColRNAI
- **Identity:** 98.5%
- **Present in:** 8/8 isolates (100%)

#### Plasmid 4: Small Col-type Plasmid (~5.6 kb)
- **Replicon types:** Col(pHAD28), ColRNAI, Col440I
- **Present in:** 8/8 isolates (100%)

### Variable Plasmids (Present in subset of isolates):

#### JKPB381 unique: ~190 kb virulence plasmid
- **Replicon types:** IncFIB(K) + IncFIB(K)(pCAV1099-114) + IncFIC(FII) + repB(KPC_VIR)
- **Significance:** This is a KpVP-1-like virulence plasmid (hypervirulence marker)

#### JKPR282 unique: IncI1-I(Alpha) + IncI(Gamma) plasmids
- **Present in:** 1/8 isolates
- **Significance:** IncI1 plasmids are broad-host-range conjugative plasmids

### Key Replicon Types Identified:
1. **IncFIB(pNDM-Mar)** - 100% prevalence - carbapenemase plasmid backbone
2. **IncHI1B(pNDM-MAR)** - 100% prevalence - co-replicon on large plasmid
3. **IncFII(pHN7A8)** - 100% prevalence - AMR plasmid backbone
4. **IncR** - 100% prevalence - co-replicon on medium plasmid
5. **ColRNAI** - 100% prevalence - small mobilizable plasmid
6. **Col440I** - 100% prevalence - small cryptic plasmid
7. **IncFIB(K)** - 11% - virulence plasmid (KpVP-1)
8. **IncI1-I(Alpha)** - 11% - broad-host-range conjugative
